Channel Lead Resources


Every 6 weeks a current student (from each of the four Milestone sections of the course), is selected as a Channel Lead for their respective channel.

Channel Leads assist other students by creating learning material in a variety of topics, and presenting via Slideshow, image or PDF uploads and/or scheduling Slack calls and webinars.

This site provides a central platform for Students to easily access these resources.
