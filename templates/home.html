Welcome to the Code Institute Channel Lead Resources platform

Every 6 weeks a current student, from each of the four main sections of the course, is selected as a Channel Lead for their respective channel.

Channel Leads assist other students by creating learning material in a variety of topics, and presenting via Slideshow, image or PDF uploads and/or scheduling Slack calls and webinars.

This site is to provide a central platform for the Code Institute Students to easily access these valuable and helpful resources.
